Srija Kotaru

Srija Kotaru is a vocalist from Dallas and a finance major at Indiana University. She has been learning Carnatic music from Rajeshwary Auntie and light music from Parthu Nemani, with whom she’s also completed two musical projects. A USA finalist for Telugu Indian Idol, she’s now moving on to the main stage. Srija is currently working on new musical projects while continuing her training and education.
