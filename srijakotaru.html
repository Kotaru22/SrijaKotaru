About Srija

Srija Kotaru is a versatile vocalist based in Dallas, Texas, renowned for her captivating performances in both traditional Carnatic music and contemporary light music genres. Her journey in music began at a young age, nurtured by a deep passion for the art form and rigorous training under esteemed gurus.

With a voice that effortlessly navigates intricate classical melodies and soulful modern compositions, Srija brings a unique blend of authenticity and innovation to her craft. She believes in the power of music to connect, heal, and inspire, and strives to create an immersive experience for her audience with every note.
